about

My name is Shannon

As a student of the School of Information at the University of Michigan, I have a passion for UX design and enjoy the creative process. In my free time I love to create, perform, and listen to music. My other hobbies include tennis and photography.

For the past 3 years, I’ve been pursuing my bachelors degree in UX design at the University of Michigan along with a minor in music. I have a background in programming and cognitive science. Because of this I have a great attention to detail, as well as an understanding of both people and technology. My education has given me a genuine appreciation for the necessity of designing with people in mind.
